Cricket my favourite game

Cricket is a bat and ball game. It is played between two teams. It is of three types tests, odis and t20s. There are infinite overs in tests, 50 overs in odis and 20 overs in t20s for each team. When the first team goes on batting the team sends two players to start the match and they are known as openers. The bowlers bowl ad they batters runs on the pitch or sends the ball out of the boundary to score points which are known as runs in cricket. Cricket can also be played as street cricket and gully cricket. Which have its own rule.

There are many events like world cups, ipl, bbl etc. These events have their own rules. India have won 2 world cups. One against Sri Lanka and one against pakistan
